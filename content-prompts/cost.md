# Template: Cost of Ownership (costs)
- Open with a concise intro of 60 words max.
- Quantify device, subscription, replacement, and coaching/opportunity cost.
- Build a mandatory verdict block with exactly 3 bullets in order:
  1) Best for: ...
  2) Avoid if: ...
  3) Bottom line: ...
- Include at least 3 FAQs.
- Mandatory CTA context: ring + $1/mo newsletter + free protocol PDF.

## E-E-A-T requirements
- Use real, verifiable prices with date-stamped sources (e.g. "$299.99 MSRP as of March 2026, manufacturer website").
- Include total cost of ownership over 1-year and 3-year horizons.
- Compare pricing tiers transparently — no hidden fees omissions.
- Cite published subscription pricing pages or official spec sheets.
- Mention resale value, warranty terms, or refund policies where relevant.
- Disclose affiliate relationships or sponsored nature if applicable.
